Fatima H.
1 day ago 📍 London, UK 💼 Virtual Assistant
📚 Essential tips for online job interviews that helped me land 3 job offers:

1️⃣ Test your internet connection beforehand
2️⃣ Have backup plans (phone, different location)
3️⃣ Practice explaining your background positively
4️⃣ Research the company's values and mission
5️⃣ Prepare examples that show resilience and adaptability

Your experience as a refugee IS valuable - it shows incredible strength! 💪
#CareerTips #Interviews #VirtualAssistant #Success
